What's my tip-of-the-week?

My tip of the week is to be aware of the "sunk cost" mindset that people get whenever they are developing solution to problems they have.
Many times, people start off with a certain methodology, and after spending a decent amount of time pursuing that solution, they realize that the solution is inherently flawed or not the best solution for the problem. Unfortunately, they are unwilling to abandon their current solution because of the amount of time they've spent on it.
Make yourself aware of the fact that the time spent on the past doesn't have any tangible value. If there is a better solution, or if he solution you currently have just isn't good for you, then you should resolve to abandon it without any second thought. Sometimes this is hard because of the emotional investment you might make with a solution, be it pride or whatever else, but if you keep wasting time because of those reasons, you'll only look back at it in the future with regret and confusion as to why you were so stubborn at the time.
