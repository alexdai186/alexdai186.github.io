What's my experience of the class?

My experience of this class was great this week. I really enjoyed the peer presentation we made in class. It was helpful trying to explain our product and its use cases to another team. It really made us grasp our own product better and helped us learn the improvements we had to make in order for it to be a helpful and useful product. On the flipside, being a consumer allowed us to also see what was and wasn't working in another team's product. From their product, we learned that embedding other multimedia aspects, such as videos and twitter feeds into our pages weren't too difficult to do, and really added a layer of depth to our own product. All in all, I really enjoyed the class this week and believed it to be super helpful the future.

What's my tip-of-the-week?

My tip of the week is directed to all of those people out there that like to waste time and watch youtube clips of movies you've already seen 100 times. That's what I did this weekend, and I feel bad for being so unproductive. One of the hardest things for me is pushing myself to do work if I'm already on schedule. I find it really hard to do stuff without the pressure of an immediate deadline breathing down my neck.
My tip is to set yourself up for productivity. It's hard to do work when you wake up at 2pm and are trying to do homework while in bed. Make sure your environment and your mindset is ok when you do it. That means waking up at a reasonable hour, and putting yourself in an environment that stimulates productivity, whether that be a library, or even just a desk where you can't lie down.
