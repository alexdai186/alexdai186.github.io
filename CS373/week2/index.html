What's my tip-of-the-week?

My tip of the week is to do all the readings before the week starts because it seems like the quizzes are less common sense stuff and just reading checks. If you can't find the time to read all of the material thoroughly, I would suggest finding friends and creating a google doc of summaries of the readings. You could potentially switch off every week so that one person does all of the readings and summarizes it for the rest of the people. This way, you can still get the big picture ideas and concepts the reading is supposed to convey in a more time friendly and collaborative way.
