What's my tip-of-the-week?

My tip of the week is to related to making guacamole. When you dice your tomatoes, onions, and garlic, it helps to put them into a food processor or a blender of some sort before you mix it in with the avacodoes. The reason for this is becaue the food processor gets the veggies really finely diced, while also squeezing a lot of juice out of the mixture.
The reason why I do this is because I used to always get bites of guacamole that was one full piece of garlic or something like that, and would ruin some of the morsels of guac I ate. By processing the veggies, you ensure that the vegetables are finely chopped. Also, the juice that comes out allows you to better distribute the flavor across the entirety of the guacamole.
Give it a try next time you are making guacamole!
